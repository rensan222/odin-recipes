Thai Coconut Soup

This soup has a lot of yummy Thai flavors and it's one of our special dishes. Served over steamed rice, you won't be disappointed with this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons red curry paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 (13.5 ounce) cans coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/2 pound fresh shiitake mushroooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

How we do it:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Add ginger, curry paste, and lemongrass
  3. Stir in the hot oil for 1 minute
  4. Stir in chicken broth, then in fish sauce
  5. Add coconut milk and mushrooms
  6. Stir them until the mushrooms are soft
  7. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro
